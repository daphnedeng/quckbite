FAQs

Answers to common questions:

What is your delivery proccess?

Menus are posted weekly. Customers may order food from the menu for delivery within that week. You will designate the delivery date on the order page. For same day delivery, orders must be placed by 11am for delivery by 5pm. Shipping is included in the price.

Where do your ingredients come from?

All ingredients are organic and produced is sourced from local farms within 50 miles.

Do you require a membership?

No membership is required, but our membership package is the most economical for those who eat with us regularly since it offers a 20% discount on all food and kitchenware.

We understand that some of our customers need flexibility. Thus, our customers are welcome to skip the membership and purchase the quantity they want when it best fits their schedule.

Do you offer vegetarian meals?

Yes, at least one meal per week will be vegetarian.

Are there shipping charges?

No. We attempt to keep the delivery process simple and transparent. Shipping is built into the cost of the meals, so no additional charges will be applied for shipping.
